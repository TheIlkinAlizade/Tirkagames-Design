About Us

Hello! We are Tirka Studio, an indie game studio based in Azerbaijan. Our team consists of 4 members: 3 artists and 1 developer. Although our studio was founded in April 2025, each member has around 2 years of experience in the game industry. We are passionate about both playing and creating games. GameJams are our biggest source of fun and inspiration. Despite being a newly formed team, we’ve already achieved several successes. Our most notable project is “My Chef Hates Me”, which won 1st place at Gamepons Jam Vol. 4. Our current goal is to publish the game on Steam.

Our Games

My Chef Hates Me!

In this fast-paced kitchen brawler, two players are connected by a rope. One is an intern trying to cook a delicious meal under pressure. The other is a chef… who absolutely hates the intern.

Doodie Moodie

The "Doodie Moodie" game is a puzzle game where in an absurd universe, a human is taken by aliens and crowned as their king, now trying to solve their problems by ridiculous ways which work but it should not.
